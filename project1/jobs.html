Data Analyst

Position Reference Number: JVC27-6498

Salary: $80,000 - $100,000 per year

As a Data Analyst at Swinova Tech, you will analyse complex datasets to generate insights that drive business decisions. You will collaborate with cross-functional teams to develop data models, reports, and predictive analytics.

Key responsibilities for the position include:

  1. Collect, clean, and analyse large datasets to identify trends and patterns.
  2. Develop interactive dashboards and reports to support business intelligence.
  3. Use statistical techniques and machine learning models for predictive analysis.
  4. Work with different teams to provide actionable insights based on data findings.
  5. Ensure data integrity and accuracy across all business reporting processes.

Essential qualifications for this position include:

  • Bachelor’s degree in Data Science, Statistics, Computer Science, or related field.
  • Proficiency in SQL or Python for data manipulation and analysis.
  • Experience with data visualisation tools like Tableau, Power BI, or Google Data Studio.
  • Strong analytical skills with a keen attention to detail.

Our ideal candidate would also possess the following qualifications:

  • 2+ years of experience as a Data analyst
  • A recommendation from industry supervisor
  • Strong understanding of cloud data solutions (AWS, Azure, or Google Cloud)

This position reports directly to Swinova Tech's Lead Data Analytics

Cybersecurity Specialist

Position Reference Number : JVC27-4755

Salary: $85,000 - $110,000 per year

As a Cybersecurity Specialist at Swinova Tech, you will be responsible for protecting our systems and data from cyber threats. You will develop security policies, conduct risk assessments, and implement defense strategies to ensure a secure digital environment.

Key responsibilities for the position include:

  1. Monitor and assess security threats, vulnerabilities, and risks.
  2. Keep the security system upto-date
  3. Develop and enforce security policies and best practices.
  4. Perform penetration testing and security audits.
  5. Respond to security incidents and conduct forensic investigations.
  6. Ensure compliance with industry regulations and standards.

Essential qualifications for this position include:

  • Bachelor’s degree in Cybersecurity, Information Technology, or a related field.
  • Experience with network security, firewalls, and intrusion detection systems.
  • Proficiency in security tools such as SIEM, IDS/IPS, and endpoint protection.
  • Knowledge of cybersecurity frameworks (NIST, ISO 27001, CIS Controls).

Our ideal candidate would also possess the following qualifications:

  • Valid Professional certification (CISSP, CEH, CISM, or equivalent).
  • 2+ years work experience in the industry.
  • Recommendation from industry supervisor
  • Strong analytical and problem-solving skills.

This position reports directly to Chief Information Security Officer
